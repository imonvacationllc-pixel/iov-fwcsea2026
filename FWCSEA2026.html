I.O.V-(Cancer) Voyage Avec Moi Afro Travel Girl Graphic Tee

Light, confident, and made to travel: this soft cotton tee shows a bold, globe-ready woman carrying her bags and inviting the world along. The illustration’s warm tones and subtle typography “Voyage avec moi...” create a feeling of quiet adventure — the kind that starts at the airport gate and stretches into unfamiliar streets, late-night cafés, and easy friendships. Wear it on travel days, city explorations, or whenever you want to feel steady, styled, and ready to go. The breathable Bella Canvas fabric and a crew neckline keep the look clean and comfortable, while thoughtful construction (side seams, shoulder tape, ribbed collar) helps the shirt hold its shape through packing and repeat wears. It reads as both relaxed and purposeful — travel energy without the fuss.

Product features
- 100% Airlume combed ring-spun cotton (lightweight, breathable)
- Retail crew neckline with ribbed knit collar and tear-away label for comfort
- Built with side seams and shoulder tape for shape retention and durability
- Prints produced using DTF/DTG methods for crisp, vibrant artwork
- REACH certified; responsibly manufactured (Bella+Canvas ethical production)

Care instructions
- Machine wash: cold (max 30C or 90F)
- Non-chlorine: bleach as needed
- Tumble dry: low heat
- Iron, steam or dry: medium heat
- Do not dryclean
